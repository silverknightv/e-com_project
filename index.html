On-ear headphones are usually more compact than over-ear designs. They have smaller ear cups that rest on the ears and also slightly less bass.

should buy on-ear headphones? Listeners who want a decently comfortable fit in a more compact design.

Over-ear headphones typically have thick headbands and large ear cups that fully encompass the ears.

Who should buy over-ear headphones? Listeners who want an easy-to-achieve comfortable fit and don't mind the larger headphone size.
